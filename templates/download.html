About the Conference:

Kairos 2025 is the 9th Annual Conference of The Issachar Generation (TIG), hosted by RCCG Praise Assembly. This year's theme, "Kairos," represents God's appointed time - a moment of divine opportunity and breakthrough.

Join us for three power-packed days of worship, word, and transformation as we explore what it means to recognize and seize God's appointed time in our lives. Experience powerful ministrations from anointed speakers, life-changing worship sessions, and divine encounters that will propel you into your season of manifestation.

Featured Speakers: Dr. Levin Damisan, Mrs. Olugbesoye Olujimi, Min. Esther Orji, Pastor O Jacob, Pastor (Mrs) Temitope Onuegbu, Min. Leke Gospel, and Apostle Jonathan Shekwonya
